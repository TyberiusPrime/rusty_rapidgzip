  1. u32 per-chunk byte cap (likely a real bug for pathological input).
  StringPod/Storage positions are u32; the builders panic!("byte buffer exceeds 
  u32::MAX"). A single 4 MiB compressed chunk of synth-zeros decodes to ~4 GiB —
  which overflows and panics on untrusted input. The speculative decoder
  pre-reserves min(est, 64 MiB) but doesn't cap actual output. → Decide: cap
  decoded chunk size, or move positions to u64, or document a max compression

  data.
